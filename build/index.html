Research

My research is in computer graphics and visualization, particularly the use of geometric algorithms to visualize geometric data. Currently, I am studying the construction of isosurfaces with sharp features. See Publications for some of my publications including my new book, "Isosurfaces: Geometry, Topology, and Algorithms". See http://www.cse.ohio-state.edu/graphics/isotable for isosurface software.
